The Akan Naming System

This is a website to determine your name based on the day of the week that you were born. This is a Ghanian system called: "The Akan Naming System".
If you are male your your name would be:

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwandwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
